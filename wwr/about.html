History

Since 1995, our crew of river rebels has been riding wild waves, making a splash in the world of white water rafting. What started as a passion project became a thrilling legacy, weaving stories of laughter, excitement, and unforgettable river escapades.

With each oar stroke, we've carved a path through the most exhilarating rapids, bringing the joy of rafting to all. Our history is a tale of endless fun, shared moments, and a sprinkle of river magic. Join us, and let's make a splash together on a river journey you'll treasure forever! 🌊😄
